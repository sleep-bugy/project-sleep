About Project Sleep

We're a passionate team of developers dedicated to creating the ultimate Android experience through innovative custom ROMs and modifications.

Our Mission

Project Sleep was born from a simple idea: Android should be beautiful, fast, and customizable. We believe that everyone deserves a premium mobile experience without the limitations of stock firmware.

Our team combines years of Android development experience with cutting-edge technology to deliver ROMs that push the boundaries of what's possible on mobile devices.

Development Timeline

Key milestones in Project Sleep's journey

Project Inception

January 2023

Project Sleep was founded with the vision of creating the ultimate Android custom ROM, combining performance, beauty, and customization.

First Alpha Release

March 2023

Released the first alpha version of SleepOS with basic features and optimizations for 5 flagship devices.

Community Launch

June 2023

Launched our Discord server and community forums, reaching 1,000 active users within the first month.

SleepOS 2.0 Release

September 2023

Major update introducing glassmorphism UI, advanced battery optimization, and support for 50+ devices.

100K Users Milestone

December 2023

Reached 100,000 active users across all supported devices and launched the HyperOS modification project.

Current Development

2024

Continuous development with Android 14 support, 150+ devices, and advanced AI-driven optimizations.
